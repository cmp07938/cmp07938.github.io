Street style from Fashion Week consists of the unique outfits that show-goers wear to stand out. These street style looks are how "it" items are created. In fashion week, if you are not photographed, you don't exist. This is why fashion-forward types can be seen in disruptive styles. They go against current consumer trends and create a new norm.

Street style is important because it is immediate and inspires consumers to buy products that are available now. The outfits are weather appropriate and supported by peers via social media. This allows for easy access and imitation.
